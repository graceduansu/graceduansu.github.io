About Me

I'm currently pursuing a Master of Science in Robotics at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Jun-Yan Zhu in the Generative Intelligence Lab. I received my Bachelor of Science from Columbia University, where I majored in Computer Science and minored in Applied Mathematics.

My main research interest is in computer vision, especially generative models, representation learning, semantic understanding, and synthetic data generation. I'm interested in improving generative models, leveraging their learned representations, and investigating how training with synthetic data affects visual representation learning.
